About Us

Coach Mitzi and Coach Shane have extensive experience in competitive swimming and the art of coaching and teaching. With 20+ years of profession, Coach Mitzi and Coach Shane stepped away as professional athletes in 2018 and started Horizon Swimming, a highly regarded swim school based in Taipei, Taiwan, with students from all over the world. Coach Mitzi and Coach Shane are currently based in Southern California and have coached students from Irvine, Hacienda Heights, Diamond Bar, Chino, Brea, Pasadena, Arcadia, and Beverly Hills. While they trained as national-level swimmers themselves and have coached many more competitive swimmers, Coach Mitzi and Coach Shane also love sharing their passion with novice swimmers. The beginner’s lessons are designed to equip young students with basic skills and safety to potentially inspire their love of swimming; the competitive swimming lessons train swimmers in advanced swimming techniques and strive to reach their full swimming potential.
